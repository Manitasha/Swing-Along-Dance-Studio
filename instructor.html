Instructors

Meet Neil

Neil D’souza is a professional dancer himself and has over 12 years of experience teaching dance under his belt.He is an expert of ballroom dances like swing, salsa, charleston, lindy-hop and balboa.He teaches both beginners and professional dancers. His classes are carefully tailored according to the crowd at hand and are interactive and playful.

Meet Stacy

Stacy is an approachable and committed Dance Teacher who is keen to share her passion for dance with those that she teaches.She likes to create a classroom atmosphere where visitors are welcome and peer observation is the norm.She gives personal attention to all her clients and is always there to guide them in going forward.
