About Us

Who we are

Swing Along Dance Studio is a one stop studio based in Seattle for all your dance needs.We take private and group dance classes, hosts dance socials and provide event choreography.Through dance we aim to build self esteem, confidence and discipline while promoting teamwork and creativity .

what we do

Swing Along Dance studio provides private and group classes for east coast swing, west coast swing, balboa, charleston, lindy-hop and other ballroom dances.We provide training to dancers at all levels - beginners, intermediate and professional.
